Projects

CCTV and Street Light

Bringing safety and efficiency to our barangay through an integrated CCTV and street light project. This initiative involves installing modern CCTV cameras equipped with advanced monitoring capabilities at strategic points across our community. Paired with energy-efficient LED street lights, this dual-purpose system not only enhances security by deterring crime and improving surveillance but also promotes environmental sustainability by reducing energy consumption and light pollution. By investing in technology that prioritizes safety and sustainability, we are committed to creating a brighter and safer future for all barangay residents.


Recycle and Waste segregation

Promoting a sustainable future through effective waste management and recycling initiatives in our barangay. Our project focuses on educating residents about the importance of waste segregation at the source. We will provide color-coded bins for easy sorting of recyclables, biodegradables, and non-recyclable materials. Regular awareness campaigns and workshops will empower community members to participate actively in reducing landfill waste and preserving our environment. By fostering a culture of responsible waste disposal and recycling, we aim to create a cleaner, healthier barangay for generations to come.
